# Seyirmatik - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 22, 2026

Seyirmatik ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension operates and handles your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
Seyirmatik does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or sensitive user information to any third-party servers.

- **Local Storage:** The extension stores minimal user preferences (such as your chosen display name and selected avatar index) locally within your browser using Chrome Local Storage.
- **Real-Time Video Synchronization:** When you join a watch party room, room playback states (play, pause, seek time) and WebRTC signaling data for peer-to-peer video calls are transmitted strictly between room participants to synchronize video playback and enable live communication.

## 2. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user data to outside parties.

## 3. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can open an issue on our GitHub repository: https://github.com/masterkayisi/seyirmatik-server
